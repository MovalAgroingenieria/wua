========================================
Pressurized Irrigation Energy Monitoring
========================================

This module adds the functionality of measuring the energy used in
pressurized irrigation.

Description
===========

In the context of well pumping installations, this module adds a set of new
functionalities related to the recording of electrical consumption, pressure
and flow measurements.

Specifically, it is about working with certain magnitudes related to the
mechanical energy that allows the water to be driven and with the electrical
energy necessary for this. Based on the measurements already made, it is
possible to obtain the energy efficiency ratio. This efficiency will be within
the range between 0% and 100%.
