=============================================
Quota Management: Superproduct Water Transfer
=============================================

Quota management added possibility to transfer water between superproducts.


Description
===========

This module adds to normal quota management the possibility of transferring
water from some supraproducts marked as transferable to others and register
two hydric movements as two individual inputs, one positive for the
receiver superproduct and one negative for the transferable superproduct.

Functionality:

* Water Transfer model created to transfer water between quotas.
