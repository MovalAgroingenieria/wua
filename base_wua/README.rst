==================================
Water Users Association Management
==================================

This module allows you to manage water users associations.

Description
===========

Management of users and parcels of water users associations.

* Parcel owners of a wua.
* Parcel leaseholders of a wua.
* Geographical and identification data of parcels.
* Etc.
