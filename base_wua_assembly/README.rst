=====================================================
Management of assemblies for Water Users Associations
=====================================================

Management of the complete process of a assembly.

Description
===========

Management of the complete process of a assembly of WUA partners.

Functionality:

* Agenda items.
* Delegation of vote.
* Attendance management.
* Vote count.
